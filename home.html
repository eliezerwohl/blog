Always time for Change....

I'm a seasonal music listener. Different bands for different seasons. For instance, during the fall, nothing reflects the falling leaves better than listening to a sweet Motorhead album. During the cold winter, nothing really brings out the bitter cold winds quite like a tasty Motorhead album. Spring in a time of rebirth. Nothing really captures nature coming back to life quite like a nasty boogie from Motorhead. And finally, the summer. A time for laughs, adventure, fun in the sun, and a most excellent time to play nothing but Motorhead tracks.
